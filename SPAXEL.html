ARS ELECTRONICA Spaxels

The term SPAXEL is derived from “Space Pixels”; SPAXELS® are visual elements positioned freely and dynamically in space. We use drones to carry a LED lighting system and combine them into a beautiful and organic swarm of airborne lights. Each individual SPAXEL is directed to its predetermined position by our central proprietary flight control unit.

more information

The Ars Electronica SPAXELS® GmbH designs and executes spectacular airborne light shows for outdoor events since 2012.

Drone 100 - Intel in collaboration with Ars Electronica Futurelab holds the world record in the category Most Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs) Airborne Simultaneously.
